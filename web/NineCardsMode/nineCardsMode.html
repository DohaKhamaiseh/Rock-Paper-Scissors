Rock Paper Scissors

Nine Cards Mode

Game Rules:

- Both you and the computer will choose one of the three options: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
- Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, and Paper beats Rock.
- If both players choose the same option, it's a tie.
- You can choose each option (Rock, Paper, Scissors) up to 3 times only during the game.
- The game consists of 9 rounds in total.
