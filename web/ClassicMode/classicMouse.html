Game Rules:



Rock beats Scissors: Rock smashes or breaks Scissors.
Paper beats Rock: Paper wraps and covers Rock.
Scissors beat Paper: Scissors cuts Paper.

Here's a summary of the outcomes:
If both players choose the same option, it's a tie.
If one player chooses Rock and the other chooses Scissors, Rock wins.
If one player chooses Scissors and the other chooses Paper, Scissors win.
If one player chooses Paper and the other chooses Rock, Paper wins.
